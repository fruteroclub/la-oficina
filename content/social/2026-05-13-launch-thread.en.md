# Thread — La Oficina launch (EN)

---

**1/7**

Today we opened the repo for **La Oficina** — the operating system of Frutero Agency.

Open source. Public from day one. Built in the open.

🔗 github.com/fruteroclub/la-oficina

---

**2/7**

Frutero is no longer just a community.

It's now two things at once:
→ A **Talent Accelerator** training AI-specialized operators.
→ An **agency** that sells their work.

Same operators we train inside are the ones who deliver outside.

---

**3/7**

The bottleneck for every agency was senior talent. Scarce, expensive, uncloneable.

AI changes the unit economics:

**1 human Architect + N AI Operators** can deliver what used to require a team of N+1 humans.

---

**4/7**

La Oficina organizes that into five levels:
agency → pod → workspace → project → workstream.

Each level carries its own AGENTS.md, INDEX.md, LOG.md.

An agent loads context with one command. Continuity lives in git, not in someone's head.

---

**5/7**

Every **pod** is led by a human Architect. Legible P&L. Attributable quality.

Operators (human or AI) deliver. The Architect sells and supervises.

It's a mini-firm inside the firm. Three pods queued up.

---

**6/7**

Our first Operator is **Dumbleclaw**.

It runs on the Hermes harness. First agent operating a Frutero pod end-to-end.

Persona vs harness: Dumbleclaw is identity; Hermes is swappable infrastructure. That separation matters.

---

**7/7**

All of it built in public. Code, decisions, mistakes, daily journals.
