# Workstream: v0.1.0-launch-content

## Goal

Produce the build-in-public content that surrounds the La Oficina v0.1.0 launch arc — the "why this setup" foundational explainer first, then the supporting pieces (launch post, thread cuts, internal education doc) as the v0.1.0 implementation progresses on the target server.

Content produced here is also context Dumbleclaw ingests to adapt its public-facing communication.

## Scope

**Includes:**
- Long-form blog posts (ES + EN) for the Frutero Club blog covering the architecture, positioning, and progress of La Oficina v0.1.0.
- Thread / short-form derivatives extracted from the long-form pieces for X/Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.
- Internal education docs aimed at Frutero Club members and prospective Operators.
- Drafts, outlines, and editorial notes living under `docs/` in this workstream.
- Source-of-truth canonical blog drafts at the project-level `content/blog/` directory (referenced via RESOURCES.md).

**Excludes:**
- Implementation of La Oficina scripts (TASK-007..015 in project TICK.md) — server-side work, not done in this workstream.
- Real client engagement work for pods 1/2/3 — lives in pod-scoped workstreams, not here.
- Naming or rebrand decisions — captured in DECISIONS.md when they arise, not decided in this workstream.

## Success criteria

1. "Por qué La Oficina" / "Why La Oficina" published (ES + EN) on the Frutero Club blog before v0.1.0 is tagged.
2. At least one short-form derivative (thread or LinkedIn post) published per canonical long-form piece.
3. Dumbleclaw has consumed the canonical "why" piece and is producing on-brand surface communication adapted from it.
4. Cumulatively, 5+ public-facing pieces shipped across the v0.1.0 arc (per NEXT-STEPS.md success criteria).
5. Every piece traces back to a specific milestone, decision, or engagement — no generic AI content.
